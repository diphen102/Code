BIG BANG

Haru Haru

Big Bang is a South Korean boy band formed by YG Entertainment in 2006. The group consists of G-Dragon, T.O.P, Taeyang, Daesung, and Seungri. Big Bang is known for their experimental music style, which incorporates a wide range of genres, including hip-hop, R&B, electronic, and rock. They have also been praised for their fashion sense and trendsetting abilities. Big Bang has released five studio albums, all of which have been commercial successes. The group has also won numerous awards, including Artist of the Year at the Mnet Asian Music Awards and Best Male Group at the Seoul Music Awards. Big Bang is considered one of the most influential K-pop groups of all time.
